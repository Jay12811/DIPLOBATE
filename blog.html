How to Write a Killer Opening Speech

Template you can adapt:

  3. Policy: 2–3 concrete points
  4. Call to action: invite collaboration

Chairing 101: Managing Moderated Caucuses

  • Announce topic & speaking time clearly
  • Prioritize new voices
  • Time‑keep strictly; rotate regions
  • Summarize themes before motions
