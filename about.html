About Diplobate

Diplobate is a youth‑run collective advancing diplomacy‑first leadership through Model UN, debate, research, and community initiatives.

Mission

To democratize access to public speaking and global literacy—so every student can negotiate, advocate, and lead.

Values

  • Respectful discourse
  • Evidence‑based thinking
  • Inclusivity & accessibility
  • Global citizenship

What We Do

We host training cohorts, curate guides, mentor secretariats, and build international collaborations under @diplobate_ and @diplobate.intl.
